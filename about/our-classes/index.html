what specifically will we teach?

  • We will be trying to teach to people of any level. Naturally most of the members will be beginners so a huge emphasis will be on the fundamentals.
  • Some of the founders of the club have a strong leg lock backgrounds so we will focus on integrating leg locks from the ground up in a safe and systematic manner.
  • Because two of the founders have strong wrestling backgrounds we will spend the beginning of classes working takedowns

class breakdown

  1. Warm up through drills and or movement training (~10min)
  2. Working floor to feet takedowns (~10min)
  3. working ground techniques (~15min)
  4. rolling (sparring) (~15-20min)
